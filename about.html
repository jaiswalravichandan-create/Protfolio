Ravi Chandan Jaiswal - Official SEO Biography

Who I Am

My name is Ravi Chandan Jaiswal, a young tech enthusiast, entrepreneur, and founder of Lurnxo, Lurnxo Careers, and Lurnxo Innovation. I was born on 28 September 2009 and I come from a small village named Samardhira in Maharajganj, Uttar Pradesh. Today, I live in Gorakhpur and continue to build my journey in tech and entrepreneurship.

Where I Come From

I come from a humble background in Samardhira village. Growing up in a small village taught me the value of hard work, dreams, and the importance of education. My mission started from my roots, where I saw many students unable to study because of financial problems.

My Father – The Key to My Journey

My father, Umesh Chand Jaiswal, is the biggest support system in my life. He is the reason I started my journey. His belief, motivation, and sacrifices inspire me every day to work harder and help other children achieve their dreams.

How I Started My Tech Journey

My tech journey began with curiosity — curiosity about how websites work, how apps are built, how businesses scale, and how I can use technology to solve real problems for students. I learned everything step-by-step: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, AI tools, UI/UX and more. Slowly, I started building projects, helping people, and creating digital solutions.

My Skills

  • Frontend Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React)
  • Backend Development (Python, Django, SQL)
  • AI Tools & Automation
  • Business Strategy & Digital Marketing
  • Branding & Startup Building

How I Started Lurnxo

Lurnxo was born from a strong vision — to make education accessible to every child in India. Later, I expanded it into:

Lurnxo Careers – Helping students find jobs, internships & skill-building pathways.
Lurnxo Innovation – A service-based branch offering branding, websites, marketing, automation & growth solutions to businesses.

Mission & Vision

My Mission: To remove the financial barriers that stop children from studying.
My Vision: “India mein koi bhi baccha paise ki wajah se padhayi se na ruk jaye.” I want every child to afford quality education, no matter where they come from.
