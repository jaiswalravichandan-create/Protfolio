Ravi Chandan Jaiswal - Full Biography

Early Life

My name is Ravi Chandan Jaiswal. I was born on 28 September 2009 in a small village called Samardhira, Maharajganj, Uttar Pradesh. Coming from a humble background, I grew up with strong values and a vision to build something meaningful.

My Father – My Foundation

My father, Umesh Chand Jaiswal, is the reason behind my journey. He believed in me when I had nothing — no resources, no proper connections — just dreams. He motivated me to start learning technology, improve myself, and follow my passion.

My Tech Journey

I started learning technology out of curiosity. Slowly, I explored programming, AI tools, business strategies, design and digital products. I built projects, helped clients, and started exploring the startup world.
