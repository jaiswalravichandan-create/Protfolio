Founder of Lurnxo – Ravi Chandan Jaiswal

How Lurnxo Started

Lurnxo started with a dream to transform education in India. I saw many students struggling because education was expensive, opportunities were limited, and guidance was missing.

Lurnxo Careers

Lurnxo Careers helps students and professionals find jobs, internships, and skill-building opportunities. It connects learners with opportunities they never knew existed.

Lurnxo Innovation

Lurnxo Innovation is the service-based branch. We offer websites, branding, marketing, automation, and creative services to businesses and startups.

The Vision Behind the Company

I founded Lurnxo because I want every child of India to have access to quality learning — without worrying about fees.
